About the Conference



U+I=UXperience STARburst is an immersive workshop and showroom
experience where innovation and creativity collide in the digital design world.

LUIMINUX is a cutting-edge digital design studio specializing in UI/UX,
immersive experiences, and innovative product development, dedicated to crafting intuitive and impactful digital solutions. By hosting U+I=UXperience STARburst, they aim to foster community, inspire bold design, and push the boundaries of digital innovation through knowledge-sharing and hands-on experiences.



Dive into the art and science of user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) design through hands-on activities, expert-led discussions, and cutting-edge showcases.

Whether you're a design enthusiast, a professional, or someone eager to break into the industry, this event will empower you to harness the latest trends and techniques shaping the future of digital experiences.
